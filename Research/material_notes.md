# Print Material Testing Log

## PLA
- Temperature: 210-230°C
- Bed: 60°C
- Layer adhesion: Excellent
- Detail: Good
- Warping: Minimal with brim

## PETG
- Temperature: 230-250°C  
- Bed: 70°C
- Layer adhesion: Very Good
- Detail: Moderate
- Warping: Moderate

## TPU (Flexible)
- Temperature: 220-240°C
- Bed: 50°C
- Best for: Gaskets, soft joints
